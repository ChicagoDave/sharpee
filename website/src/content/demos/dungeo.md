## About Dungeon

Dungeon is a faithful recreation of the original Mainframe Zork from 1981. This demo showcases Sharpee's capabilities for building classic parser-based interactive fiction.

### Features Demonstrated

- **Natural language parsing** - Understands complex commands like "put the sword in the case" and "examine all"
- **Rich world model** - Containers, supporters, light sources, and locked doors
- **NPC interactions** - The infamous troll and thief
- **Scoring system** - Find treasures and put them in the trophy case

### Commands to Try

Once you start the game, try these commands:

- `look` - Describe your surroundings
- `examine mailbox` - Look more closely at something
- `open mailbox` - Interact with containers
- `take leaflet` - Pick up objects
- `read leaflet` - Read text on objects
- `inventory` - See what you're carrying
- `go north` or just `n` - Move around
