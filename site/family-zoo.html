Family Zoo Tutorial

Family Zoo is Sharpee's tutorial game: a small zoo adventure with rooms, objects, NPCs, puzzles, timed events, and scoring.

The Current Tutorial: The Sharpee Book (v2.0.0)

For Sharpee 2.2, the Family Zoo tutorial lives in The Sharpee Author and Developer Manual. Its 31 chapters build the zoo from a single room to a complete, themed, transcript-tested browser game — custom actions, capability dispatch, scenes, daemons, scoring and victory, channels, theming, media, and walkthrough testing. Every listing was verified against the published packages by a literal front-to-back read. Chapter snapshots are on GitHub under tutorials/familyzoo/v2.0.0.

The Zoo in Chord

The whole zoo also exists in Chord, Sharpee 3.0's story language — rooms, NPCs, timed events, custom actions, and scoring as one declarative zoo.story file. If you know the zoo from the book, reading it in Chord is the fastest way to learn the language.

The Legacy Web Tutorial (Sharpee 1.x)

The original 17-step web tutorial below was written for the 1.x line and is kept for readers on those versions. Each step adds one concept — by the end you'll have a complete game.

The Game

Seven rooms: Zoo Entrance, Main Path, Petting Zoo, Aviary, Gift Shop, Supply Room, Nocturnal Exhibit

Key objects: zoo brochure, zoo map, animal feed, souvenir penny press, flashlight, disposable camera, backpack, lunchbox

NPCs: a patrolling zookeeper ("Sam"), a squawking parrot, pettable goats and rabbits

The goal: Earn 100 points by visiting exhibits, feeding animals, pressing a souvenir penny, taking photos, and staying after hours.
