## Special Mechanics

### Light Sources
- Brass lantern (limited battery)
- Ivory torch (permanent once lit)
- Pair of candles (limited duration)
- Matchbook (very brief, multiple uses)

### Vehicles
- Inflatable boat (river navigation)
- Basket (vertical shaft transport)
- Balloon (volcano area) [Zork II content?]

### State-Dependent Objects
- Dam (open/closed affects reservoir/river)
- Boat (inflated/deflated/punctured)
- Egg (opened/unopened, method of opening matters)
- Rainbow (solid/insubstantial)
