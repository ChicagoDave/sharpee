Please use a laptop or desktop

Sharpee multi-user rooms are designed for full keyboards and wider screens. Phones and small tablets can't fit the live-typing panel, chat, and participant roster.

Why desktop-only?
Multi-user Sharpee lets everyone watch a shared typist's keystrokes stream live. That means a keyboard in hand, fast reactions, and enough vertical space to read long story output — not a thumb-typing session on a phone.
