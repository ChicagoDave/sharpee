# Chapter subtitle proposals (`Title: Subtitle`)

A stab at a colon subtitle for every chapter, in the format you set (`Title: Subtitle`,
no em dash). Aim: descriptive and modest, naming the chapter's hook — not grand
("paradigm") language. Three kinds of entry below:

- **CONVERT** — chapter already has an em-dash or `&` subtitle that should become a colon.
- **NEW** — chapter has a bare title; proposed subtitle added.
- **DONE** — already in the new format (Ch 15), shown for reference.

Mark up / cut any that feel forced. Some short chapters (Light & Dark, Scenes) may read
better with no subtitle at all — your call.
