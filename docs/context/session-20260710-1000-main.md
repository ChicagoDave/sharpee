- Prior art: ADR-116 (Prompt-to-Playable, Proposed) bets on AI spec→code generation; ADR-185 (Accepted) fixes the author audience as standalone npm projects against published packages.

Never implemented beyond the ADR-140 helpers slice. Predates ADR-189 defaults, world.connectRooms, phrase algebra maturity.
- devkit `sharpee init` template teaches the raw API, not helpers — first-contact experience is the rawest form.
- Evidence: zoo petting action = ~60 lines of four-phase + capability-dispatch boilerplate for 3 sentences of intent; authors hand-build ISemanticEvent literals and daemon getRunnerState/restoreRunnerState plumbing; message-ID constant maps must be kept in sync by hand across files.
