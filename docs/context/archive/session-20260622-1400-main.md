## Key Decisions

### 1. Use `grammaticalNumber` (not `nounType`) as the plural signal
`nounType: 'plural'` is the article-selection signal (already present on plural entities set to `article: 'some'`). Using it for verbs would be conflating two concerns. `grammaticalNumber: 'plural'` is the dedicated structural flag and leaves article behavior unchanged.

### 2. Exclude gerund/reflexive verb lines from the sweep
Four lines whose verb agrees with a gerund subject rather than the entity placeholder ("Pulling X has no effect", "Pushing X has no effect", "Talking to yourself is ...", inventory comment) were explicitly excluded. Changing them would produce grammatically incorrect output ("Pulling X have no effect.").

### 3. Version bump strategy â€” user-owned
Version bumps are present in the working tree (`lang-en-us` 1.0.9 â†’ 1.0.10; `helpers` 1.0.8 â†’ 1.0.9) but nothing is committed. Full republish of â‰ˆ23 README-touched packages plus `lang-en-us`, `helpers`, and the pending `devkit` 1.0.10 (from the 0700 session) is user-managed post-session.
