## Key Decisions

### 1. No consolidation of scope systems
The three systems serve different callers at different stages of the turn cycle. They form a deliberate pipeline — merging them would couple systems that need to stay independent. Document and clarify rather than collapse.

### 2. Rename path over document-and-close
Two unrelated classes named `ScopeEvaluator` was genuine confusion worth fixing in code, not just in comments. Renaming to `RuleScopeEvaluator` and `GrammarScopeResolver` makes their roles unambiguous at the call site.

### 3. StandardScopeResolver.canSee() duplication deferred
`StandardScopeResolver.canSee()` reimplements logic already in `VisibilityBehavior.canSee()`. This is real duplication but does not cause bugs today. Fixing it is a future follow-up item, not in scope for ISSUE-065.
